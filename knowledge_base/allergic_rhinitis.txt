Disease: Allergic Rhinitis

Overview:
Allergic rhinitis is an allergic reaction causing inflammation of the nasal passages after exposure to allergens.

Symptoms:
- Sneezing
- Runny nose
- Nasal congestion
- Itchy eyes
- Watery eyes
- Cough

Causes:
Pollen, dust mites, mold, pet dander.

Risk Factors:
- Family history
- Asthma
- Exposure to allergens

Diagnosis:
Clinical evaluation and allergy testing.

Treatment:
Antihistamines, nasal corticosteroids, avoiding allergens.

Prevention:
Reduce allergen exposure, use air filters, maintain indoor cleanliness.

Emergency Signs:
Difficulty breathing, severe swelling of the face or throat.
