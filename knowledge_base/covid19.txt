Disease: COVID-19

Overview:
COVID-19 is a viral respiratory disease caused by SARS-CoV-2. It ranges from asymptomatic infection to severe pneumonia and respiratory failure.

Symptoms:
- Fever
- Dry cough
- Fatigue
- Shortness of breath
- Loss of taste
- Loss of smell
- Sore throat
- Headache
- Body pain

Causes:
Transmission occurs through respiratory droplets, aerosols, and close contact with infected individuals.

Risk Factors:
- Older age
- Diabetes
- Heart disease
- Obesity
- Chronic lung disease

Diagnosis:
PCR testing, rapid antigen testing, chest imaging, and clinical evaluation.

Treatment:
Supportive care, antiviral medications, oxygen therapy when necessary, and hospitalization for severe cases.

Prevention:
Vaccination, wearing masks during outbreaks, hand hygiene, and maintaining physical distance.

Emergency Signs:
Difficulty breathing, persistent chest pain, confusion, bluish lips, or low oxygen levels.
