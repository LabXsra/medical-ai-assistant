Disease: Asthma

Overview:
Asthma is a chronic inflammatory disease that causes narrowing of the airways, leading to breathing difficulty.

Symptoms:
- Wheezing
- Cough
- Chest tightness
- Shortness of breath

Causes:
Genetic factors, allergies, exercise, pollution, smoke, respiratory infections.

Risk Factors:
- Family history
- Allergies
- Smoking
- Air pollution

Diagnosis:
Spirometry, lung function tests, allergy testing.

Treatment:
Inhaled bronchodilators, corticosteroids, avoiding triggers.

Prevention:
Avoid allergens, smoke, pollution, and follow prescribed medications.

Emergency Signs:
Severe wheezing, inability to speak full sentences, bluish lips.
